### `name`

Title of right

### `type`

Type of right

| Вид      | Объяснение                                                   |
|----------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| `TRUE`   | Available to any player                                      |
| `FALSE`  | Available if issued separately (e.g. with LuckPerms)         |
| `OP`     | Available for server operators                               |
| `NOT_OP` | Available if issued separately (e.g. with LuckPerms)         |
