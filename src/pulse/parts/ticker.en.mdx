### `ticker`

Updating every specific interval of time
<Callout type="info" title="How to use the ticker?">
    <Steps>
        <Step>
            Replace `false` with `true`
        </Step>
        <Step>
            Restart `FlectonePulse` using the command `/flectonepulse reload`
        </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout type="info" title="">
        Additional parameters for configuring the delay will appear automatically
    </Callout>
    #### `enable`

    Whether to update

    #### `period`

    How often to update, measured in [ticks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Tick)
</Callout>
