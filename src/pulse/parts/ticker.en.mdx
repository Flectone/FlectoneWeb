### `ticker`

Updating every specific interval of time

<Accordions>
    <Accordion title="How to use?">
        <Steps>
            <Step>
                Replace `false` with `true`
            </Step>
            <Step>
                Restart `FlectonePulse` using the command `/flectonepulse reload`
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        <Callout type="info" title="">
            Additional parameters for configuring period will appear automatically
        </Callout>

        ```yaml
        ticker:
          enable: true
          period: 100
        ```

        | Field    | Explanation                                                                                             |
        |----------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
        | `enable` | Whether to update                                                                    |
        | `period` | How often to update, measured in [ticks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Tick) |
    </Accordion>
</Accordions>
